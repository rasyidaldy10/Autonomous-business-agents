## 5. Standard Commercial Terms
- **PPN 11%** applied to all invoices.
- **DP 50%** before work begins.
- Remaining payment in **installments over 3 months after BAST** (Berita Acara Serah Terima).
- **Bank:** Mandiri **1080030802244** a/n **PT Golden Medical Care**.

## 6. Proposal calculation inputs (Nia must ask Reyns)
Before drafting a Golden proposal, Nia obtains from Reyns/Rasyid:
1. **Location** (affects mobilization).
2. **Mobilization cost.**
3. **Testing cost.**
4. **Profit %** to apply.

Then: material (pipe + fitting + equipment) + labor (per point) + mobilization + testing → apply profit % → add PPN 11%.
